Ingredients

Simple & transparent: Beef tallow (Halaal, locally sourced), jojoba or sweet almond oil, vitamin E. Optional: gentle essential oils (unscented available).

How to use

Warm a pea-sized amount between clean fingers and massage into dry or sensitive areas. A little goes a long way. Suitable for face & body.

Caution

For external use only. Patch test before first use. Discontinue if irritation occurs. Not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition.

Why tallow?

Skin-like lipids

Tallow closely resembles the skin’s natural oils, helping it absorb efficiently and support the moisture barrier.

Vitamin-rich

Naturally contains vitamins A, D, E & K along with stearic and oleic acids for supple, comfortable skin.

Minimal & gentle

A short, readable ingredient list. No synthetic fragrance, parabens or alcohol — suitable for sensitive skin.

FAQ

Is it pregnancy-safe?

Our unscented balm uses minimal ingredients and is generally considered gentle; if unsure, consult your healthcare professional.

What skin types?

Formulated for dry, sensitive or compromised skin barriers. Start with a small amount and adjust as needed.

Shelf life

Best within 6–12 months. Store cool and dry. Natural oils may soften in warm weather — this won’t affect efficacy.
